What services do you offer?+
Thumbnail design, poster/flyer design, logo & brand identity, video editing, social media content, and Blender 3D.
How long does a project take?+
Thumbnails: 1–2 days. Logos: 3–5 days. Full brand identity: 2–3 weeks. Video editing: 2 days to 2 weeks depending on length and complexity.
What are your rates?+
Thumbnail design starts at $20. Poster from $20. Logo from $30. Full branding from $70. Contact me for a custom quote.
Do you work with international clients?+
Yes, absolutely. I work with clients worldwide. Communication via email, WhatsApp, or video call. Payments via bank transfer, Wise, or Payoneer.
How many revisions do I get?+
I offer revisions until you're satisfied. Most projects finalize in 2–3 rounds. A clear brief at the start minimizes back-and-forth significantly.
What files will I receive?+
PNG, JPG, PDF for design work. AI/PSD source files available on request. Videos delivered in MP4/MOV in your required resolution.
